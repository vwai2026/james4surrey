MEET JAMES YU

James (Jian) Yu has spent years serving Surrey through community organizations, conservative riding associations, and healthcare foundations. He knows this city — its strengths, its challenges, and its people.

As Chairman of the Canadian Chinese Conservative Alliance and a director of both provincial and federal riding associations, James has the experience, the relationships, and the conservative values to fight for Surrey families on City Council.

LEARN MORE

LEADERSHIP PRIORITIES

DEFEND PUBLIC SAFETY

捍卫公共安全

Surrey residents deserve safe streets and real consequences for criminal activity. James will oppose drug proliferation, support evidence-based rehabilitation, and ensure law enforcement has the resources to keep families safe.

SOUND FISCAL POLICY

健全财政政策

People who work hard should be able to get ahead. James supports reduced property tax burden, transparent spending, faster project approvals, and responsible fiscal management.

PROTECT FAMILY VALUES

守护家庭价值

Surrey families need a city that works for them. James will champion accessible childcare and senior care, defend parents' rights in education, and build a livable city where every generation can thrive.
